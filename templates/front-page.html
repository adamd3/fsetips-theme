What is Full Site Editing?

Full Site Editing (FSE) represents a fundamental shift in how WordPress websites are built and customized. It brings the block editor's flexibility to every part of your site.

With FSE, you can visually design your entire website - from headers to footers, and everything in between - using a consistent block-based interface.
